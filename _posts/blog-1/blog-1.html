COVID-19 Italy

This blog post explores the current situation of COVID-19 in Italy.

XITONG HE https://xitong-covid19-italy.netlify.app (Monash University)
09-04-2020

Figure 1: Italy response to coronavirus. Source from:Harvard Business Review

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease and has been become a pandemic in the world. Italy has experienced the COVID-19 virus outbreak, especially for March, the situation was out-of-control. In this post, I am going to analyze the case situation for this epidemic in Italy.

Data description

There are three data sets used on this analysis, and the cleaned data is obtained from GitHub RamiKrispin. In addition, the covid19italy package is created by Rami Krispin Krispin (2020) as well which is provided a tidy format dataset of the 2019 Novel Coronavirus COVID-19 (2019-nCoV) pandemic outbreak in Italy.

The three datasets are shown as below:

  • italy_total - daily summary of the outbreak on the national level

  • italy_region- daily summary of the outbreak on the region level

  • italy_province - daily summary of the outbreak on the province level

These three datasets is collected the information from 2020-02-24 until now, updating daily data information. Overall, Italy has the 269214 total case of COVID-19 until now.

Analyse

Figure 2 shows the number of active, recovered, death and total case in each region in Italy from 2020-02-24 until now. By interacting the table, Lombardia has the most of cases in all attributes and the death case are more than 10,000 which is almost the twice of Piemonte. The reason for this phenomenon with highest death in Lombardia due to the possible delayed public health response, high urban density and considerable elderly population which is difficult to control transmission between asymptomatic individuals at the community level so it makes the situation worst than other regions Odone et al. (2020). And Basilicata has the less cases in all 21 regions,which has been announced early to the travelers who has to pre-register by filling out a designed form which shows where they will be staying and when will they depart, as part of efforts to halt the spread of the Coronavirus pandemic (COVID-19).

Figure 2: COVID-19 case in different region in Italy

The overall cases cross time in Italy as displayed in the Figure 3. In general, there are 269214 confirmed cases, 207653 recovered case and 35483 deaths case. The active cases in Italy have a massive upsurge which are shown in the February and March, marking the biggest coronavirus outbreak outside Asia. In addition, the death rate and recovery rate have soared in March compared with February and after that the death rate has stabilized at 14%. In April, the active cases continue to rise sharply and after a peak then flat out at the end of this month. Started from May to July, Italian government took a series of measures such as the social distancing and travel restriction to significantly ease the spread of the coronavirus Gatto et al. (2020). Because of these strict measures, the active cases have gradually decreased as well as the recovery rate have increased rapidly after April as many patients have been cured. However, from the line graph in August, the active cases represent a gradually growth, the reason may be raised by holidays and night life for causing people to gather in numbers from some experts explained. Due to the active case has rise significantly again, the recovery rate has drop slightly as well.
